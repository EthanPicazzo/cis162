One Piece starts off with a very old animation style but gets very polished and more of a round way of drawing characters. Most of the characters have this whacky flair making them all look unique.
Full Metal Alchemist: Brotherhood has a very old artstyle and kept it through the series. All the characters have very sharp edges to their faces and most have the same body type.
Narruto, like One Piece, started off with a old style of animation but got a lot more smoothed out. The villians and most side characters are unique but most background characters kinda fall under a generic look.
Tokyo Ghoul has a very elegant animation style with some very flashy moments that set it apart from the rest. The anime used a bit of CGI but overall solid animation.
